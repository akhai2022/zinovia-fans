## POST-PRODUCTION NOTES

### Transitions
- Use **smooth zoom** transitions between app screens
- **Speed ramp** (slow → fast → slow) when scrolling through the homepage
- **Quick cuts** (0.3s) for the problem section and trust section
- **Fade to black** between major sections

### Text Animation Style
- Use **pop-in** or **slide-up** for text overlays
- Font: **Space Grotesk** (matches the app's display font) or Inter
- Color: White text with subtle drop shadow, gradient pink for keywords
- Always overlay on semi-transparent dark bar for readability

### Color Grading
- App screens: boost contrast slightly, make the pink/purple pop
- Stock footage: slightly desaturated to contrast with the vibrant app UI
- Consistent dark background feel throughout

### Sound Design
- Subtle **whoosh** on transitions
- Soft **click** sound on button interactions
- **Cha-ching** sound effect on the earnings dashboard scene
- Beat-synced cuts throughout
